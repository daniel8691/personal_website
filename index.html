About Me

From a Hotelier to the Field of Analytics...

Going into University, my goal was to enjoy the school life and graduate with a job working at a hotel/resort. This mindset was, for the most part, inspired by my love for travelling and facing new challenges as I relocate to new environments. During my school years, I have worked in Jasper, AB and St. Andrews, NB. I would treasure these experiences because they significantly shaped my character in a positive way, especially with personal confidence.
During my last year of study at the University, I developed an interest in entrepreneurship and the idea I had was to create a platform for ridesharing, that addresses common issues within this market. That is when I came across data analytics which I found as a crucial skill to have in the long run because I could then figure out the types of data I should collect from clients, and bring data to life by creating visualizations and building machine learning models.
I chose to attend a Bootcamp program because I wanted to learn from a structured curriculum that will teach me the knowledge needed to succeed in the data analytics field. As expected of the bootcamp, the fast-paced curriculum, complemented with direct interactions with teaching instructors/assistants, accelerated my learning efficiency. Fast forward 7 months, I am now able to develop Deep learning projects from scratch from using Python’s web-scrapping tools, to data manipulation, to data visualizations to using sequence prediction techniques to uncover any trends and seasonality within the dataset! .
