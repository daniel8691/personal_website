About Me

From Hotel Management to the Field of Analytics...

Going into University, my goal was to enjoy school and graduate with a job working at a hotel or resort. This mindset was, for the most part, inspired by my love for travelling and adapting to different challenges as I relocate to new environments. During this time, I have worked in Jasper, AB, St. Andrews, NB, and Collingwood, ON. I treasure these experiences not only because of the incredible scenery and supportive community there, but also because I was able to develop into a confident, inquisitive, and outgoing person .

During my last year of undergrad study, I developed an interest in entrepreneurship. The idea I had in mind was to create a platform for ridesharing, that addresses common issues within the market. That is when I came across data analytics, which I found as a crucial skill to have in the long run. Using analytical techniques, I could figure out the the best characteristics to analyze across different demographics, and bring data to life by creating visualizations and building machine learning models.

I chose to attend a Bootcamp program because I wanted to learn from a structured curriculum that will teach me the knowledge needed to succeed in the data analytics field. As expected of a bootcamp, the fast-paced curriculum, complemented with direct interactions with teaching instructors/assistants, accelerated my learning efficiency. Fast forward 7 months, I am now able to develop deep learning projects from scratch using Python's web-scrapping tools, create advanced data visualizations by manipulating databases, and uncover trends and seasonality within datasets using sequence prediction techniques!
